Our Mission & Promise

Candles with purpose. Light that speaks.

Every One Flame™ candle burns for more than light and fragrance — it burns for truth. With each purchase, a portion of the proceeds goes toward:

  • Hiring trusted, compassionate legal help for those facing government overreach
  • Supporting efforts to end human trafficking
  • Helping veterans who have fought for this country but now face poverty

When you light a One Flame™ candle, you’re standing with people who demand justice, who demand freedom — and who demand the truth, now.

This is more than a candle; it’s a call to action. Together, one flame at a time, we shine a light on injustice, honor our heroes, and fight for a world where truth and freedom prevail.

Transparency

We publish quarterly impact reports with donation receipts and partner updates so you can see the difference your flame made.

How Funds Flow

A portion of net proceeds supports our three pillars. We balance immediate relief with long‑term programs.
